✨ About SkinLesionAI

SkinLesionAI is a deep learning–based research project developed for educational and academic purposes. It classifies skin lesion images into multiple categories, supporting the study of dermatology and AI in healthcare.

🎯 Motivation

Inspired by the idea of exploring how AI can help analyze both normal and abnormal skin lesions, not only cancerous ones. The goal is to show how AI can support researchers, students, and developers in dermatology.

📂 Acknowledgments

Trained on publicly available dermatology datasets such as ISIC 2019. Thanks to the open-source community for providing valuable resources.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is for research and educational purposes only. It is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified dermatologist for concerns.
